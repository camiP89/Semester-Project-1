Energetica exhibition on loan

dates:

6th December 2024

20th December 2024

On loan from the NEMO Museum in Amsterdam, The Energetica Exhibition is coming to the Community Science Museum. It's a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the power of the Elements as we harness them. From solar powering lighting, to 'Wind Island' that shows how turbines are able to use and control wind to create power.
